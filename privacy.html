Summary: Block Star Journey is a puzzle game that respects your privacy. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the game experience, show relevant ads, and save your progress. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Block Star Journey ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile puzzle game Block Star Journey (the "Game") available on Android.

By downloading, installing, or playing the Game, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information Collected Automatically

When you play the Game, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), screen resolution, and device language settings
  • Game Progress Data: Levels completed, scores, stars earned, achievements, and in-game purchases
  • Usage Data: Session duration, features used, game events, and crash reports
  • Advertising Data: Ad interactions, impressions, and clicks (for showing relevant ads)

B. Information You Provide

  • Player Name: Optional display name you choose for your profile
  • Cloud Save Data: If you enable cloud saving, your game progress is stored securely
  • Support Requests: If you contact us, we collect the information you provide

C. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real name, address, or phone number
  • Email address (unless you contact support)
  • Photos, contacts, or location data
  • Financial information (payments processed by app stores)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Game: Save your progress, sync across devices, and deliver game features
  • Improve the Game: Analyze gameplay to fix bugs, balance difficulty, and add features
  • Show Advertisements: Display relevant ads to support the free game experience
  • Process Purchases: Verify and deliver in-app purchases (IAP)
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and requests
  • Analytics: Understand how players interact with the Game

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract Performance: To provide the Game and its features you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve the Game, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Consent: For personalized advertising (where required by law)
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Sharing Summary

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device and optionally in the cloud

We retain your data for as long as:

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Block Star Journey is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction, such as 16 in some EU countries).

  • We do not require personal information to play
  • Ads shown comply with children's advertising guidelines (kid-safe ads when applicable)
  • We do not use behavioral advertising for users identified as children
  • If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete the information within 48 hours

Parents/Guardians: If your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us at support@glimmergridgames.com.
